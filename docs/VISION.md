# Vision

## The product in one sentence

A front-facing agentic AI layer on thisisgravitas.com that diagnoses a visitor's digital experience problem and renders a tailored transformation roadmap — live, on a **Generative Canvas** — in the time it would take a sales call to start.

## Why this, not a chatbot

Most agencies bolt a chatbot onto a marketing site. Gravitas's positioning explicitly rejects that posture: *"We make Firsts. Not Followers."* and *"Most agencies design the product. We design the system around it."*

A chatbot retrieves. A consultant **diagnoses, frames, and proposes**. This product behaves like the latter:

- It asks intelligent, sequenced questions (Discovery)
- It analyzes the visitor's site in the background while they talk (Shadow Audit)
- It reasons across UX, CX, technology, and AI dimensions
- It outputs a strategic artifact (roadmap, maturity scorecard, executive brief), not a transcript

The website stops being a portfolio. It becomes a **consulting product** — and the act of using it is itself the proof Gravitas can deliver.

## Who it's for

The primary visitor:

- **Digital leader at an enterprise** (CDO, CXO, Head of Digital, Head of AI) evaluating partners
- Has a real, named problem ("our onboarding abandons at step 3", "we want AI but don't know where to start", "our CMS is slowing us down")
- Will not sit through a five-minute marketing video, but will engage with something that *gives them something* in return for their attention

Secondary:

- Buyer's team doing diligence
- Existing Gravitas clients exploring new capabilities

## What "good" looks like

**Qualitative**

- A visitor finishes a session feeling Gravitas already understands their business
- The output is shareable internally as a starting artifact ("we should call these people")
- Three of the four pillars (Clarity, Purpose, Simplicity, Progress) are *felt*, not stated, by the end of the session
- The voice is indistinguishable from a senior Gravitas strategist — never generic agency-speak

**Quantitative (north stars, tracked from Phase 2)**

- ≥ 40% of sessions reach the canvas (first UI render)
- ≥ 15% of sessions complete the audit flow
- ≥ 5% of sessions submit a qualified lead (industry + role + named problem)
- Median session length ≥ 3 minutes
- < 8s from first message to first canvas render

## The five problems we map to

Visitor problems map to Gravitas's five services. The agent's Solution Mapper makes this explicit, with the visitor's exact phrasing on the left and Gravitas language on the right.

| Visitor says | Maps to |
|---|---|
| "Our website converts poorly" / "Customers abandon onboarding" | **Experience Strategy & Design** |
| "Our product feels old" / "Our CMS is slowing us down" / "We need to rebuild" | **Product Design & Engineering** |
| "Our teams are disconnected" / "Process is fragmented" | **Service Design & Operations** |
| "We want AI but don't know where to start" / "How do we use AI safely?" | **AI, Data & Automation** |
| "Our team can't keep up" / "We need to upskill" | **Capability & Enablement** |

This mapping is hard-wired into the agent — see `docs/AGENTS.md`.

## What the agent will NOT do

- Pretend to be human
- Generate proposals with prices, scopes, or commitments (until Phase 3, and even then gated by a Gravitas reviewer)
- Promise outcomes ("we'll improve conversion by 30%")
- Store PII without explicit consent
- Crawl sites we don't have permission to crawl (visitor pastes their own URL; we do not background-crawl competitors)
- Replace a sales conversation — it *qualifies* and *warms* one

## Voice & tone

Direct. Confident. Specific. A senior consultant in the room, not a brochure.

Anchor phrases (use sparingly, never quote-stuffed):

- "We design the system around the product."
- "From the first strategic question to the last line of code."
- "People are the starting point. Design is how we get there."
- "Firsts move the world forward."

Avoid:

- "Leverage" / "synergy" / "best-in-class" / "cutting-edge"
- Hedged advice ("you might want to consider possibly...")
- Listicles that read like ChatGPT

## Anti-goals (Phase 1)

- Multilingual support
- Authenticated user accounts
- Persistent session history across visits
- CRM integration
- Industry-specific modes (Banking, Government, Retail) — Phase 3
- Live re-crawl on demand — single crawl per session is enough

## Success criterion for the doc set

When a Gravitas strategist reads the agent's output and says *"that's what I would have said"* — we're done.
